Monster Gallery

Twisted remnants of humanity and malfunctioning machines, the creatures haunting PATHOS-II are the tragic byproducts of the WAU, an artificial intelligence tasked with preserving life at any cost. In its misguided attempt to “save” what remains of humanity, the WAU fuses flesh with machinery, copying consciousnesses and warping bodies into grotesque hybrids. Each monster reflects a different form of suffering—echoes of human minds trapped in mechanical shells—serving as both predator and a grim reminder of what it means to lose one's humanity.
